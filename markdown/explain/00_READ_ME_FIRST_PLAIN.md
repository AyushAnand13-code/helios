# Helios in Plain English — Start Here

Friendly, jargon-light companions to the dense technical docs. Read these to
**understand fast and build**; open the precise technical doc/PDF only when you
need exact SQL, schemas, or field contracts.

Each guide keeps the exact canonical names (so you learn the real vocabulary)
but explains every one of them in everyday language.

## Read in this order

**1 — Orient (what & why)**
- `START_HERE_PLAIN` — the map and the 4-week targets.
- `HELIOS_PROJECT_BIBLE_PLAIN` — the whole product in one read. The big one; start here for the "why".
- `LEAN_SCOPE_PLAIN` — what's IN vs OUT of the first minimal build.

**2 — Plan the build (order & dependencies)**
- `HELIOS_EXECUTION_GUIDE_PLAIN` — your daily playbook and exact first task.
- `DEPENDENCY_MAP_PLAIN` — what depends on what; the build order.
- `DEVELOPMENT_PLAN_PLAIN` — milestones and their exit gates.
- `IMPLEMENTATION_PLAYBOOK_PLAIN` — step-by-step, milestone by milestone (M0–M12).

**3 — Build the data spine**
- `DATA_MODEL_PLAIN` — the tables, the funnel, the grains of record.
- `DBT_GUIDE_PLAIN` — how raw GA4 events become clean funnel tables.
- `METRIC_DEPENDENCY_GRAPH_PLAIN` — how metrics derive from one another.
- `METRIC_GOVERNANCE_GUIDE_PLAIN` — why metrics are defined once, in one place.

**4 — Build the AI layer**
- `MCP_ARCHITECTURE_PLAIN` — the five tool servers the AI is allowed to call.
- `AGENT_ARCHITECTURE_PLAIN` — the seven agents and how they hand off work.

## How to read these

- PDFs are under `pdf/explain/` — that's your reading copy.
- Each guide ends with a **"When to open the real doc"** pointer to the precise
  version (and its PDF) for when plain English isn't enough.
- These are companions, not replacements. The technical docs remain the source
  of truth.
